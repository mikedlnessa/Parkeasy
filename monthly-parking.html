FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for monthly parking?

You can sign up online by selecting your preferred location and plan, then completing the registration form. You'll need to provide your vehicle information and payment details.

Can I cancel my monthly parking plan?

Yes, you can cancel your plan at any time. We require 30 days notice for cancellations. There are no cancellation fees.

What happens if my parking spot is taken?

With our Premium plan, your spot is reserved exclusively for you. With other plans, we guarantee availability but not a specific spot. If a lot is full, we'll direct you to the nearest available location.

How do I access my parking facility?

After registration, you'll receive a digital access card in our mobile app. For gated facilities, you can use this card or your license plate will be recognized by our automated system.
